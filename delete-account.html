Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your Volcast account and all associated data

Your data, your choice. Volcast allows you to delete your account and all associated data at any time, either directly from the app or by contacting us via email.

Option 1: Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the Volcast app:

  1. Open the Volcast app on your device
  2. Go to the Settings tab (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion manually, send an email to:

Email: hello@volcast.app
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your Volcast account so we can locate and delete your data. We will process your request within 3 business days and send a confirmation email once completed.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, all of the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

Data Category Details
Account & profile Email, display name, language, preferences
PV system configuration Panel strings, inverter settings, location
Production data Manual production entries, calibration logs
Forecast data Cached forecasts, forecast snapshots
Notification settings Alert preferences, push tokens (all devices)
Appliance preferences Smart scheduling device selections
Purchase data RevenueCat subscriber record removed
Authentication Auth user account permanently deleted
This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. If you purchased Volcast Premium, your purchase history is retained by Apple/Google as part of their transaction records, but your Volcast subscriber profile is removed.

Data Retained by Third Parties

While we delete all data from our own servers, certain third-party services may retain minimal records according to their own policies:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — purchase transaction records (managed by Apple/Google, not by Volcast)
  • Crisp Chat — if you contacted support, your chat history may be retained under Crisp's data retention policy

We do not control data held by these third parties. You may contact them directly to request deletion under their respective privacy policies.
